FAQ

How is the engagement rate calculated? +

We calculate engagement rate by dividing the total number of likes and comments by the number of followers, then multiplying by 100.

Can I check private accounts? +

No, this tool only works with public Instagram accounts.

How accurate is the data? +

We pull the most recent public data available. Accuracy depends on the visibility and availability of the account’s data.
